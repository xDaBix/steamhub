Getting Started

Follow these steps to get your game on Steam and make the most of our platform.

Step 1: Create a Steamworks Account

Sign up for a Steamworks account to access all the tools and features you need to publish your game on Steam.

Register Yourself

Step 2: Set Up Your Game

Configure your game's store page, upload assets, and prepare your game for release.

Step 3: Testing

Test your game thoroughly using Steam's testing tools to ensure a smooth launch.

Step 4: Launch

Release your game to the public and start engaging with your community.
